Why this doesn't blow up an account

Risk control is the
feature, not the disclaimer.

→

Per-trade caps

No single order can exceed a fixed share of portfolio equity, regardless of how confident a signal is.

→

Daily loss ceiling

Once a set drawdown threshold is hit in a session, new entries pause automatically until the next day.

→

Volatility-tiered sizing

Blue-chip pairs get full allocation; volatile alt pairs are automatically sized down.

→

Paper mode by default

Every deployment starts in simulation. Live trading is a deliberate, explicit opt-in.

A plain-language note

This is a trading tool, not a promise of profit. Cryptocurrency markets are volatile and automated strategies can lose money, including in ways backtests don't anticipate.

GainzAI's job is to apply your rules and risk limits with more consistency than a human can manage under stress — not to eliminate market risk. You retain full custody of funds through your own Coinbase account, and can pause or disable trading at any time.

We recommend running in paper-trading mode until you've reviewed the signal history and are comfortable with how the system behaves.
